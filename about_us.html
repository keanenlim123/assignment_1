About Us

Our Mission


Our mission is simple: to help you turn your game ideas into reality. We believe that anyone with a passion for gaming and storytelling can learn to build games, and we're here to make that process accessible, enjoyable, and effective.

Our Values


We encourage students to think outside the box and experiment with their ideas. Game development is a team effort, and we value collaboration both in and out of the classroom. We're passionate about games and the process of making them, and we want to ignite that same passion in our students.

Our Goals


Our primary goal is to build a community of creative individuals who thrive on innovation and collaboration. We aim to nurture talent, foster creativity, and inspire a new generation of game developers to push the boundaries of interactive storytelling and design.

Our Team

Eleanor Hartwell


Eleanor has over 15 years of experience in the gaming industry, having worked as a lead programmer for AAA titles like Galaxy Tactician and Shadow Realms. She holds a Master's degree in Computer Science with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence for games. Eleanor emphasizes hands-on learning, encouraging students to build games.

Marcus has developed award-winning games like Dreamscapes and Puzzleverse. Sophia Mendez

She holds a Ph.D. in Human-Computer Interaction and has collaborated with VR companies. Sophia teaches students to develop VR experiences using while focusing on storytelling and interactivity in 3D spaces.
